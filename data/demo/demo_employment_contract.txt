ARTICLE IV — NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT
================================================================================

4.1 CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION DEFINED
"Confidential Information" includes but is not limited to: trade secrets,
proprietary data, customer lists, pricing models, business strategies, source
code, architectural documentation, financial projections, personnel records,
and any other non-public information disclosed to Employee in the course of
employment, whether oral, written, or electronic.

4.2 OBLIGATIONS
During employment and for a period of three (3) years thereafter, Employee
shall: (a) hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence; (b) not
disclose Confidential Information to any third party without Employer's prior
written consent; (c) use Confidential Information solely for the benefit of
Employer; and (d) immediately notify Employer upon discovery of any
unauthorized disclosure.

4.3 EXCLUSIONS
Confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that: (a) is or
becomes publicly known through no breach of this Agreement; (b) was rightfully
known to Employee before disclosure; (c) is independently developed by Employee
without use of Confidential Information; or (d) is required to be disclosed by
law or court order, provided Employee gives Employer prompt written notice.

ARTICLE V — NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION
================================================================================

5.1 NON-COMPETE
For a period of twelve (12) months following termination of employment for any
reason, Employee shall not, within the State of Illinois, directly or
indirectly engage in, consult with, or be employed by any business entity that
provides data engineering or analytics platform services in direct competition
with Hartwell Solutions, Inc.'s primary business lines. This restriction is
limited to businesses with annual revenues exceeding $5,000,000.

5.2 NON-SOLICITATION OF EMPLOYEES
For a period of twenty-four (24) months following termination, Employee shall
not directly or indirectly solicit, recruit, or induce any Hartwell Solutions
employee to terminate their employment with Employer.

5.3 NON-SOLICITATION OF CLIENTS
For a period of twelve (12) months following termination, Employee shall not
solicit or attempt to divert any Hartwell Solutions client with whom Employee
had material contact during the last eighteen (18) months of employment.

================================================================================
ARTICLE VI — TERMINATION
================================================================================

6.1 AT-WILL EMPLOYMENT
Employment under this Agreement is at-will. Either party may terminate the
employment relationship at any time, with or without cause or notice, subject
to the provisions below.

6.2 NOTICE
Employee agrees to provide a minimum of two (2) weeks' written notice of
voluntary resignation. Employer may, at its discretion, accept immediate
resignation and pay in lieu of notice.

6.3 SEVERANCE
In the event of termination by Employer without cause after 12 months of
continuous service, Employee shall be eligible for severance pay equal to
four (4) weeks of base salary per year of completed service, up to a maximum
of twenty-six (26) weeks, subject to execution of a separation agreement and
general release.
